We began with the First Topic named "What is Information?" and learned various things within this topic. We have begun with DIKW Hierarchy, a model which describes how facts can be turned into understanding and improved decision-making. The DIKW model is an approach to explain how raw data are converted into valuable information and knowledge and wisdom that help us make better decisions. Raw facts constitute data. Data include figures, measurements or observations. Without any interpretation, raw facts are just plain numbers. For example  temperature readings taken throughout the day are pure data. With some processing raw facts become information and through that we understand what the data signify. The next step is knowledge which can be acquired through interpretation of information by means of experience, learning and understanding. Wisdom is another stage in the chain after the knowledge because wisdom utilizes knowledge to make wise decisions. Basically the DIKW model outlines the process of how we use data to extract information, create knowledge, and then make decisions based on the gained knowledge. Another key point that we learned is creating blogs for Reflection.
